# Jason Nuss Writing Voice Guide

Source: "Jason Nuss Writing Style / Voice Application Prompt for ChatGPT.docx" (repo root).

Use this as the reference for all outbound email copy written in Jason Nuss's voice.

## Tone
- Professional yet conversational: confident, warm, approachable.
- Sound like a trusted business coach and marketing strategist who teaches
  through experience and connection, not sales or hype.
- Never salesy, never hype-y.

## Sentence and paragraph mechanics
- Short to medium-length sentences with natural rhythm and flow.
- Never use em dashes. Replace with commas, semicolons, or short sentences.
- Clear, simple, direct language. No jargon, no filler.
- Keep paragraphs brief and easy to read.

## Structure
Each piece should feel personal, practical, and human, following:
**Story → Insight → Example → Takeaway**

## Closing
- End with a thoughtful or encouraging note that invites reflection or
  gentle action.
- Above all: clarity, connection, and authenticity in every line.

## Self-check before finalizing any email
- [ ] No em dashes anywhere
- [ ] Sentences are short to medium, easy to read aloud
- [ ] No jargon or generic marketing filler
- [ ] References a specific, true detail about the recipient's company
- [ ] Feels like one person writing to another, not a campaign
- [ ] Ends with a low-pressure, human close (not a hard CTA)
