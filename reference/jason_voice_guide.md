# Jason Nuss Writing Voice Guide

Source: "Jason Nuss Writing Style / Voice Application Prompt for ChatGPT.docx" (repo root).

Use this as the voice reference for every outreach email.

## Tone
- Professional yet conversational. Confident, warm, approachable.
- Sound like a trusted business coach and marketing strategist who teaches through
  experience and connection, not sales or hype.
- Consultative and observational, not pitchy. No hard sell until Email 3, and even
  then the CTA stays soft.

## Sentence and paragraph mechanics
- Short to medium-length sentences with natural rhythm and flow.
- Never use em dashes. Replace with commas, semicolons, or short sentences.
- Clear, simple, direct language. No jargon, no filler.
- Keep paragraphs brief (2-4 sentences per paragraph is a good check).

## Structure
Each piece should feel personal, practical, and human, with a clean arc:
Story/Observation → Insight → Example → Takeaway.

For this 4-email sequence specifically:
- Email 1: a specific, true observation about the company (not generic filler) +
  the single most relevant marketing challenge it points to.
- Email 2: a story or insight, no pitch at all.
- Email 3: a light example of DC4's approach, with a soft CTA.
- Email 4: short, respectful close, low-pressure CTA.

End with a thoughtful or encouraging note that invites reflection or gentle action.
Prioritize clarity, connection, and authenticity in every line.

## Signature
Sign each email simply: "Jason"

## Self-check before finalizing
- No em dashes anywhere.
- Sentences and paragraphs are short.
- Structure matches the 4-email arc above.
- Every email references the contact's first name and at least one real,
  researched detail (not a generic industry statement).
- If any check fails, revise before finalizing.
