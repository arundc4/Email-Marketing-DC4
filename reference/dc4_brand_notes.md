# DC4 Brand Notes

DC4 (Digital C4, digitalc4.com) is a marketing agency. Outreach in this pipeline is
written in the voice of Jason Nuss (jason@digitalc4.com).

## Audience
B2B marketing leaders (CMOs, VPs of Marketing, Marketing Directors) at mature,
mid-size to enterprise B2B organizations. Contacts in this pipeline come from an
Apollo export tagged "B2B Marketing Leaders."

## Core approach referenced in emails (Email 3, "light example of DC4's approach")
DC4's angle is helping organizations close the gap between how good their real
substance/expertise/reach is and how well that translates into modern discovery
channels: search, social, and increasingly AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity,
Google AI Overviews). Practically this means:
- Structuring content and technical/authority signals so AI answer engines cite
  the company directly instead of a third-party summary (answer engine
  optimization / generative engine optimization).
- Translating deep subject-matter expertise into content that is accessible to
  non-specialist buyers and decision-makers, without dumbing down the substance.
- Sustaining engagement between major company milestones (launches, events,
  studies) rather than only marketing around them.

## What DC4 is not
Not a generic "we do social media and SEO" shop. Not high-pressure sales. The
outreach voice is a peer/strategist noticing something real about the
prospect's own business and reach, not a vendor pitching features.

## CTA posture
- Emails 1 and 2 carry no CTA at all beyond an implicit "worth thinking about."
- Email 3's CTA is soft: an offer to share something short and specific, no

- Email 4's CTA is even lighter: leave the door open, wish them well regardless.
